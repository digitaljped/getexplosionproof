# Custom Explosion Proof Air Conditioners

Not every application fits neatly into a standard product offering. Some installations require unusual mounting, higher capacity, redundant cooling, specialty materials, or integration with blast-resistant or modular structures. That's where custom fabrication comes in.

We've built explosion-proof cooling systems for applications that most HVAC companies wouldn't know how to quote — and we've done it enough times that "custom" doesn't mean "slow" or "unpredictable." It means we design and build what your specific installation requires.

---

## When You Need Custom Work

**Redundant and N+1 systems** — Mission-critical applications where cooling loss means equipment shutdown or damage. We configure two units where one serves as automatic backup if the primary fails. We've built fully redundant N+1 10-ton systems and two-stage 20-ton units for space-constrained applications including conex box and tractor-trailer installations.

**Stainless steel enclosures** — Pharmaceutical facilities, food processing environments, and highly corrosive chemical environments often require stainless steel construction. We fabricate stainless enclosures to meet both hazardous location classification and material compatibility requirements.

**Blast-resistant modules (BRMs)** — Modular buildings designed to withstand overpressure events have specific HVAC integration requirements. We work with BRM manufacturers to ensure cooling equipment is properly integrated into the module design — mounting, penetrations, and classification compliance.

**Non-standard mounting** — Rooftop, pad mount, overhead, recessed, or custom bracket configurations that don't match standard wall-mount or split system installations. If you have a mounting challenge, we've probably solved something similar before.

**High-capacity systems** — Applications above 5 tons that require multiple units, staged cooling, or integration with building management systems. Refinery control buildings, large equipment shelters, and data centers in classified areas often fall into this category.

**Existing equipment modification** — You already have a unit on site and need it brought into hazardous location compliance. We can modify equipment from any manufacturer — Bard, Trane, Carrier, Lennox, or others — to meet your required classification.

## Any Brand, Any Classification

We're not limited to a single manufacturer or product line. Our independence as a specialist distributor means we can work with whatever equipment best fits your application:

- Any brand of air conditioner can be modified
